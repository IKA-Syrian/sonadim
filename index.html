Why Study in Turkey

Studying in Turkey offers unique opportunities for international students as it is distinguished by cultural and scientific leadership through study programs in Turkish universities

  • Turkey is characterized by a high-quality educational system accredited by international organizations including the Higher Education Quality and Accreditation Agency (HEA) in Europe
  • Turkish universities offer a wide range of study programs in Turkish, English, and other languages
  • International students in Turkey have the opportunity to train in their specializations during their studies and to be employed in international companies within educational international contracts

Blue Diploma

The Blue Diploma is a university document jointly accredited by European educational institutions, UNESCO, and is an international university document distinguished by its design and recognitions in line with international higher education standards, making it acceptable worldwide.

  • Possibility to continue studies at European Union universities
  • Employment opportunities within global companies in European countries
  • Intensive practical training that provides students with professional experience during their study years
Inquire about universities offering the Blue Diploma now

Student Exchange

Student exchange within the Erasmus and Molana programs between Turkish and international universities offers a unique opportunity for students from all countries to study in a new environment and learn a new culture, providing academic exchange and broadening educational horizons for students during their study years.

  • Cultural and academic exchange with international students at European Union universities
  • Different practical laboratory training specializing in medical, engineering, and social sciences

Scholarships

Our team, supported by Turkish universities and educational institutions, offers a variety of scholarships for international students, including partial, full payment, and diverse scholarships provided by Turkish universities.

  • Turkish scholarships are characterized by accepting international students regardless of their nationality or socio-economic status
  • Turkish scholarships provide high-quality education including practical applications of university specializations
  • Scholarships support international students in enhancing their job prospects after graduation
Inquire now about scholarships at Turkish universities

Student and Research Clubs

Student clubs at Turkish universities are an essential part of university life, contributing to the enhancement of professional and scientific experience and meeting the interests and passions of students, ranging from cultural and artistic clubs to sports, social, and volunteer clubs.

  • Diverse clubs that meet and develop various artistic, technical, physical, and social skills of students
  • Turkish universities monitor student clubs and provide logistical support to ensure the development of students' skills
  • Clubs provide logistical and scientific support and create an appropriate environment for developing diverse student skills
