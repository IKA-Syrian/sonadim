FAQ

Based on our experience in studying in Turkey, completing university studies, and registering in private Turkish universities and strategies for studying government university entrance exams, we have selected the most prominent inquiries that may face students with answers to support them in academic counseling

Our team strives to support students in achieving their academic dreams by guiding them in choosing the most suitable university and specialization for them, providing university admission opportunities in Turkish universities, and offering them innovative and effective study plans to help them pass the tests. We are here to create a bright future together!

Academic consultation is the first step in the journey of university studies in Turkey, where we study the applicant's file in our team and choose the best suitable solutions for them, which allow them to obtain the most suitable universities and specializations for their academic goal, then we follow up with the applicant the steps of translating and certifying the necessary documents at the Turkish government until they apply to the decided universities and specializations and obtain preliminary acceptance in a short time, which allows the student to choose from multiple university options available

Turkish universities are distinguished by international recognition where most universities have local and global recognition, such as Istanbul University, which enjoys local and global recognition, in addition to Nişantaşı, Biruni, and Gelişim universities, which are recognized by Arab countries such as Jordan, Iraq, and Palestine, and also have recognition from European Union countries. In addition, Turkish universities enjoy Mudek, FEDEK, and Tepdad recognitions, which prove the evaluation and review of the quality of academic programs as these organizations work to ensure quality and excellence in university education in Turkey

Turkish universities support their students during the study period with employment offices for integration into the labor market, and thanks to international recognitions and educational institutional contracts in Turkey, students have broad opportunities in international labor markets, including engineering, technology, management, and education fields

Registration in Turkish universities requires fulfilling the basic educational documents of the student where the documents required by Turkish universities include: acceptance letter, valid passport or national ID card, original high school certificate, high school transcript, equivalence certificate. 4 personal photos. Payment receipt. And supported certificates (optional)
