The Blue Diploma | Comprehensive Article

The Blue Diploma is a document jointly prepared by national institutions, the European Commission, and the European Centre for the Development of Vocational Training (CEDEFOP). Its purpose is to facilitate mobility and recognition of vocational and technical qualifications between European countries and global educational institutions.

Your passport to academic horizons

The Blue Diploma is based on a common European reference framework for qualifications (EQF), a system that links qualification levels and national and European institutions, facilitating the recognition of academic qualifications. The Blue Diploma also uses the European Credit System for Vocational Education and Training (ECVET), a system that allows for the transfer and accumulation of educational outcomes achieved in different educational settings.

Benefits of the Blue Diploma

- The Blue Diploma contributes to simplifying the understanding of postgraduate certificates and enhancing their comparison at the regional and international levels, thus enhancing graduates' access to local and international job and education opportunities.

- The Blue Diploma supports its holder in completing their studies at European and global universities.

- The Blue Diploma proves that the university studied at complies with European Union standards, supporting its holder in their applications to global companies and institutions.

What does the Blue Diploma contain?

The Blue Diploma includes information about the diploma holder, starting from the name of the specialization in both the original and translated languages, explanations of the curricula and diploma level, in addition to the content of education, program details, study units, grading system, ECTS credits, and information about job opportunities available after obtaining the diploma.

Terms and conditions for obtaining the Blue Diploma

Despite the importance of the Blue Diploma for those aspiring to complete their studies at international universities or work within global companies, there is no specific way to apply for this diploma, as methods and conditions vary from medical specialties to engineering specialties.


The basic requirement for the diploma is that you must have completed your department within the academic calendar, where if the duration of your study program is 4 years, you must graduate within this period.
