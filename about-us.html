About Us

Our educational teams were formed as a result of the efforts of an integrated educational team aimed at achieving radical changes in the field of education in general and supporting students wishing to complete their studies in Turkish universities in Turkey in particular, believing in our vision and academic goals

We believe in the youth's potential to create a bright future and form the last step of reaching their academic goals

  • Our Approach

We aim through our institution to provide solutions for admission to Turkish universities, and to provide clear academic steps for students wishing to study in Turkey

University admission in less than 48 hours

Through our advisory team, our internal educational system, and the academic registration process, we always aim to organize our students' files, complete them, and secure university admission in a timely manner

Scholarships available throughout the academic year

Thanks to our contracts with educational institutions and Turkish universities, we have full scholarships - pre-paid and partial scholarships available through SONADIM institution

Public university solutions

We support students of university entrance exams through dozens of work papers and distinctive trial tests, in addition to an advisory team and several packages for sorting in public universities

We aim in our missions to support studying in Turkey by providing the most suitable educational loans in Turkish universities

Integrated institutional system

Available in the internal system within our vision, an academic approach to ensure no randomness regarding student files. It runs like an algorithm starting from educational consultations, searching for the best opportunities for our students, and following up on student affairs

Supporting the builders of the future

We believe that everyone who seeks knowledge has the right to pave the way to their future regardless of the different student categories and their life circumstances. From this standpoint, the student files department uniquely studies each student's case to provide what suits their academic goals.

We welcome the student through several stages, the first of which is understanding their needs and university goals, then their student file is studied within an academic methodology to choose the most suitable university and specialization for them and the best ways to be accepted in them.

Monitoring and tracking

We follow up on our students and their university admission cases step by step, with direct communications with universities to solve any problem that may face our students' files or stand as a barrier between them and their acceptance in Turkish public and private universities

Our students' goals come first

Through our professional approach, we strive to maintain our students' goals and provide university admissions in the most suitable specializations and the closest Turkish universities to their goals, in addition to providing exclusive scholarships

SON ADIM Educational Institution

An educational institution accredited by more than 45 Turkish educational institutions, we aim to provide educational solutions and studies through a team of education experts and academic consultants in private and public Turkish universities with over 4 years of practical experience
